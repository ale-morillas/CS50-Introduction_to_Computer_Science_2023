Sports

Basketball, my favorite sport. I think the first contact I had with it was NBA Live 2003, from there my passion for basketball was born. I remember that the Lakers team caught my attention, with Kobe Bryant and Shaq as the stars of that team, at that time I was 7 years old. Since then I've been wearing the yellow and purple #LakeShow. As a teenager I started to practice it with my friends, we invented a hoop with wood and a bent steel bar to play, I averaged 6'0'' with 15 years old, so I was the best in my neighborhood. One of the best moments of my life related to this sport was to see the Lakers win in the 2020 finals against Buttler's Heat, to see my favorite player, Lebron James lift the championship trophy and the MVP award. I should also mention that I am a huge fan of the NY Knicks.

Soccer, the king of sports. One of my favorites and the most played in Cuba at the moment. My first approach to it was in the 2006 FIFA World Cup when I saw Brazil play with Ronaldinho, Adriano, Kaka, etc., since that year I have not missed a world cup. It is also the sport I practice the most along with basketball. My teams are FC Barcelona and the Brazilian National Team, and my favorite player Ronaldinho.

Baseball, the national sport of my country. I think it's the first thing your parents teach you to play, throw the ball, hit it and catch it. It was the first sport I played. In Cuban neighborhoods, children and young people play a variant called "cuatro esquinas", which consists of hitting a shaved tennis ball with your closed fist, it is played without gloves and the bases are the corners of the block. Baseball in Cuba is played semi-professionally, with one competition, the National Baseball Series, of which Industriales is my favorite team. In the MLB my team is the NY Yankees and my favorite player Ichiro Suzuki.
